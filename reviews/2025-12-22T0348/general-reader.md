## Top 3 Strengths:

1. **Exceptional clarity in explaining complex technical concepts**
2. **Honest about limitations and uncertainty**
3. **Compelling democratic vision**

## Top 3 Weaknesses:

1. **Inconsistent treatment of your own projects** - Chapters 5-9 sometimes read like promotional material
2. **Speculative chapters (11-13) need stronger framing** between proven vs. hypothetical
3. **Some repetition and structural issues** - Chapters 2-3 overlap significantly

## Recommendation:

For a general educated reader: This is a **4/5 book**. Fascinating, important, mostly accessible, but with structural and framing issues that prevent it from being a must-read.

Tighten the structure, clearly separate proven from speculative work, add one killer real-world case study.
