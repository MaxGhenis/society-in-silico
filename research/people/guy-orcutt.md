# Guy Orcutt

**Born**: 1917
**Died**: 2006
**Role**: Father of microsimulation

## Key Contributions

- "A New Type of Socio-economic System" (1957) - founding paper of microsimulation
- DYNASIM model development (1970s)
- Shifted focus from aggregate macro models to individual-level simulation
