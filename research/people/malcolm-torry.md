# Malcolm Torry

Director of Citizen's Basic Income Trust. Extensive microsimulation research on UBI.

## Roles

- Director, Citizen's Basic Income Trust
- Senior Visiting Fellow, LSE Social Policy Department
- Visiting Fellow, Institute for Policy Research, University of Bath
- General Manager, Basic Income Earth Network (BIEN)

## Microsimulation Work

Nearly 10 years of microsimulation research on basic income using [[euromod]].

### Key Findings

- Meaningful Basic Income achievable at 23% basic rate
- Can avoid most net disposable income losses
- Takes substantial households off means-tested benefits
- Substantially reduces poverty and inequality

### Publications

- "Static Microsimulation Research on Citizen's Basic Income for the UK" (EUROMOD Working Paper EM13/19, 2019)
- "Recovery Basic Income" microsimulation (ISER, April 2020)

## Books

- *Money for Everyone: Why We Need a Citizen's Income* (2013)
- *Why We Need a Citizen's Basic Income* (2018)
- *The Palgrave International Handbook of Basic Income* (2019, editor)
- *101 Reasons for a Citizen's Income* (2015)

## Significance

Demonstrates sustained use of open microsimulation tools (EUROMOD) by civil society advocates, not just academics/government.
