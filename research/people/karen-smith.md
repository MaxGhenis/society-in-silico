# Karen Smith

Senior Fellow at Urban Institute. 30+ years developing microsimulation models.

## Role

Senior Fellow, Tax and Income Supports Division, Urban Institute. Also affiliated with Tax Policy Center.

## Expertise

"Internationally recognized expert in microsimulation" with 30 years experience developing models for:
- Social Security
- Pensions
- Taxation
- Wealth and savings
- Labor supply
- Charitable giving
- Health expenditure
- Student aid
- Welfare reform

## Key Model Development

- **[[mint]]**: Lead role in developing SSA's Modeling Income in the Near Term
- **[[dynasim]]**: Lead role in Urban's Dynamic Simulation of Income Model

## Collaborators

Barbara A. Butrica, Eric J. Toder, Caroline E. Ratcliffe

## Significance

One of the most experienced microsimulation practitioners in the US. Her career spans the evolution from mainframe models to modern systems. Bridges retirement policy (MINT, DYNASIM) and tax policy (TPC).
