# John Sabelhaus

Chief of Long-Term Modeling at CBO who built CBOLT. Now at Federal Reserve.

## Current Role

Chief of Microeconomic Surveys Section, Federal Reserve Board. Primary responsibility for Survey of Consumer Finances.

## CBO Work

- Chief of Long-Term Modeling at Congressional Budget Office
- Oversaw development of integrated micro/macro model of Social Security and Medicare
- Key developer of [[cbolt]] methodology

## Key Contributions

- Developed "age-centred regression" approach for microsimulation
- Co-authored foundational CBOLT technical papers (2004-2006)
- Published in International Journal of Microsimulation

## Publications

- "Overview of the Congressional Budget Office Long-Term (CBOLT) Policy Simulation Model" (CBO Technical Paper 2004-1)
- "Projecting Labor Force and Earnings in CBO's Long-Term Microsimulation Model" (2006)
- IJM paper on age-centred regression with Lina Walker

## Significance

One of the key architects of US government dynamic microsimulation for Social Security policy analysis.
