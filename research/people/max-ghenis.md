# Max Ghenis

**Role**: Author, Co-founder & CEO of PolicyEngine, Founder of UBI Center

## Career Timeline

### Education
- **UC Berkeley** - B.A. in Operations Research and Management Science
- **MIT** (2020) - M.S. in Data, Economics, and Development Policy

### Google (2010-2018)
- Data scientist for 8 years
- Co-founded People Analytics Data Science team
- Worked on YouTube product side, helping serve users in low and middle income countries

### UBI Center (2019-present)
- Founded as open-source think tank researching universal basic income
- Produced 30+ research works on UBI from multiple angles
- Key finding: $200/month child allowance would lift a million mothers out of poverty
- Analyzed Andrew Yang's Freedom Dividend
- Incubated PolicyEngine

### PolicyEngine (2021-present)
- Co-founded with [[nikhil-woodruff]]
- CEO role
- Mission: compute the impact of public policy for the world
- Grew from UK model to US, now expanding to Canada

## Personal & Values

- Focuses giving on GiveDirectly and GiveWell
