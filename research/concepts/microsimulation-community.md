# Microsimulation Community Infrastructure

Professional associations, journals, and conferences.

## International Microsimulation Association (IMA)

- **Website**: [microsimulation.org](https://www.microsimulation.org/)
- **World Congress**: Every 2-3 years
  - 9th Congress: Vienna, January 2024 (170+ participants, 150+ presentations)
  - Hosted by Austrian Institute of Economic Research (WIFO)
- **European Meeting**: Annual
  - 2025: University of Galway, June 24

## International Journal of Microsimulation (IJM)

- **Status**: Official peer-reviewed journal of IMA
- **URL**: [microsimulation.pub](https://www.microsimulation.pub/)
- **Impact Factor**: 0.67 (2024), Q2 quartile
- **Trend**: Rising (+52% from 2023)

## Centre for Microsimulation and Policy Analysis (CeMPA)

- **Institution**: University of Essex
- **Maintains**: [[ukmod]], formerly [[euromod]]
- **Role**: Major academic hub for European microsimulation

## Key Figures in Community

- **Thor O Thoresen** (Statistics Norway): LOTTE, IJM special issue editor
- **Cathal O'Donoghue** (University of Galway): Dynamic microsimulation surveys
- **Martin Spielauer** (WIFO): microWELT platform
- **Holly Sutherland** (Essex): EUROMOD, UKMOD pioneer

## Significance

The microsimulation community is small but well-organized. The IMA and IJM provide venues for methodological exchange that cut across government, academic, and think tank practitioners.
