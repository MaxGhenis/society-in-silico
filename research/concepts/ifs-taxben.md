# IFS TAXBEN

The Institute for Fiscal Studies' tax and benefit microsimulation model for the UK.

## Institution

Institute for Fiscal Studies (founded 1969 by Will Hopper, Bob Buist, Nils Taube, John Chown)

## History

- **Started**: 1983
- **Data**: Family Expenditure Survey (now Living Costs and Food Survey)
- **Sample**: ~7,000 UK households

## Key People

- Howard Reed had primary responsibility 2000-2004 (later founded [[landman-economics]])
- Documentation by Giles & McCrae (1995), Waters (2017)

## Purpose

Budget analysis, distributional impact assessment. The IFS Green Budget analyses rely heavily on TAXBEN.

## Significance

The foundational UK tax-benefit model. Set the standard for independent budget analysis. Many UK microsimulation practitioners trained on or with TAXBEN.
