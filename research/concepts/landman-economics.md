# Landman Economics / Howard Reed

UK economic consultancy and its Tax-Transfer Model (TTM).

## Founder

**Howard Reed** - one of the UK's leading microsimulation specialists

### Career Path
- MSc Economics, University College London
- Institute for Fiscal Studies: primary responsibility for [[ifs-taxben]] (2000-2004)
- Chief Economist & Director of Research, IPPR (2004-2008)
- Founded Landman Economics (2008)
- Currently also Professor at Northumbria University

## The Tax-Transfer Model (TTM)

- **Origins**: Written 2008-09 for IPPR and Resolution Foundation
- **Evolution**: Became the IPPR model, then evolved into independent Landman TTM
- **Data**: Family Resources Survey, UKHLS, Living Costs and Food Survey

## Notable Applications

- Universal Basic Income modeling (10+ years of work)
- Cumulative impact assessments of welfare reforms (2010-2015 and beyond)
- Equality and Human Rights Commission analyses
