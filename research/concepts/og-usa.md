# OG-USA

**Institution**: Policy Simulation Library (PSL)
**Type**: Open-source overlapping-generations macroeconomic model
**Language**: Python

## What It Is

OG-USA is an open-source, large-scale overlapping-generations model of the US economy. It models how tax and fiscal policy changes affect macroeconomic outcomes (GDP, capital, labor) by simulating cohorts of agents making lifetime decisions about work, savings, and consumption.

## Significance

Bridges the micro-macro divide discussed in Chapter 1. While microsimulation models like Tax-Calculator handle distributional effects, OG-USA captures macro feedback effects (how tax changes affect growth, interest rates, wages). Part of the PSL ecosystem.
