# SPSD/M (Canada)

Statistics Canada's Social Policy Simulation Database and Model.

## Institution

Statistics Canada

## History

- **Started**: 1985
- **Current version**: Version 30 (database year 2018)
- **Type**: Static accounting model

## Components

1. **SPSD (Database)**: Non-confidential, statistically representative database of Canadian individuals in family context
2. **SPSM (Model)**: Calculates federal and provincial taxes and transfers using legislated or proposed algorithms

## Access

- Free with signed Licence Agreement
- Available through Data Liberation Initiative (DLI) for academics
- Requires significant experience to master

## Users

Federal departments, provincial governments, universities, interest groups, corporate divisions, private consultants

## Significance

"In wide use by policy analysts in Canada studying virtually every change to the tax and transfer system since 1985."

One of the longest-running government microsimulation models. Demonstrates that governments can provide accessible modeling tools while protecting confidentiality.
