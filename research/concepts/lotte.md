# LOTTE (Norway)

Norwegian tax microsimulation model system, celebrating 50 years in 2024.

## Institution

Statistics Norway (Statistisk sentralbyrå)

## History

- **Started**: ~1974 (50 years as of 2024)
- One of the oldest continuously running tax microsimulation systems

## Documentation

"The LOTTE System of Tax Microsimulation Models" by Zhiyang Jia, Bodil M Larsen, Bård Lian, Runa Nesbakken, Odd E Nygård, Thor O Thoresen & Trine E Vattø (2024), International Journal of Microsimulation.

## Special Recognition

International Journal of Microsimulation published special issue "50 Years with LOTTE" (2024), edited by Thor O Thoresen.

## Significance

Demonstrates long-term sustainability of government microsimulation infrastructure. One of the earliest examples alongside [[spsdm]] (Canada, 1985) and [[ifs-taxben]] (UK, 1983).
