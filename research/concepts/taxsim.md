# NBER TAXSIM

Internet-accessible tax calculator maintained by the National Bureau of Economic Research.

## Creator

**Daniel Feenberg** (Princeton PhD 1980) has maintained TAXSIM his entire career at NBER. Born in St. Louis, educated at Washington University and Princeton.

## Coverage

- Federal: 1960-2023
- State: 1977-2018

## Key Features

- Open access, internet-based since early days
- Users provide survey data, NBER returns tax calculations in seconds
- Used by government agencies (BLS, Census Bureau)

## Documentation

Feenberg & Coutts (1993), "An introduction to the TAXSIM model," *Journal of Policy Analysis and Management* 12(1): 189-194.

## Significance

One of the few long-running, publicly accessible tax calculators. Demonstrates that open tax modeling is possible and valuable for research.
